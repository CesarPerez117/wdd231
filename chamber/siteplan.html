Caribbean Tourism & Trade Chamber - Site Plan

Site Name

Caribbean Tourism & Trade Chamber
Domain: caribbeanchamberdr.org

Purpose

The Caribbean Tourism & Trade Chamber exists to promote tourism, trade, and economic collaboration across the Dominican Republic and the wider Caribbean. The site will:

  • Provide resources for local businesses in tourism and trade.
  • Connect entrepreneurs with investors and international partners.
  • Showcase cultural events and festivals.
  • Encourage sustainable tourism and eco-friendly practices.

Audience

  • Local Businesses: Hotels, restaurants, artisans, exporters.
  • Tourists & Visitors: Looking for authentic Caribbean experiences.
  • Investors & Trade Partners: Exploring opportunities in Dominican and Caribbean markets.
  • Community Members: Interested in cultural events and chamber initiatives.

Branding

Logo: A stylized wave and palm tree symbolizing Caribbean tourism and trade.

Colors:

  • Ocean Blue (#0077b6) → Trust, professionalism
  • Sunset Orange (#f77f00) → Energy, vibrancy
  • Sand Beige (#f4e1d2) → Warmth, hospitality

Fonts:

  • Headings: Montserrat (modern, bold)
  • Body: Open Sans (clean, readable)

Style Guide

  • Navigation: Top navigation bar with links to Home, About, Membership, Events, Directory, Contact.
  • Layout: Responsive grid system with card-based sections.
  • Imagery: Caribbean beaches, cultural festivals, local businesses, trade fairs.
  • Tone: Professional yet welcoming, emphasizing collaboration and cultural pride.

Content Outline

  • Home Page: Hero banner with tagline (“Connecting Tourism & Trade in the Caribbean”), featured events, quick links.
  • About Us: Mission, vision, leadership team, history of the chamber.
  • Membership: Benefits, pricing tiers, application form.
  • Events: Calendar of cultural festivals, trade expos, networking events.
  • Business Directory: Searchable list of member businesses with contact info.
  • News/Blog: Updates on tourism trends, trade opportunities, and chamber initiatives.
  • Contact Page: Form, email, phone, office location map.

Wireframe (Conceptual)

  • Header: Logo + navigation.
  • Hero Section: Large banner with Caribbean imagery and tagline.
  • Main Content: Grid layout for events, directory highlights, and news.
  • Footer: Contact info, social media links, copyright.
