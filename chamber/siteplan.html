Caribbean Tourism & Trade Chamber - Site Plan

Site Name

Caribbean Tourism & Trade Chamber

Purpose

The Caribbean Tourism & Trade Chamber exists to promote tourism, trade, and economic collaboration across the Dominican Republic and the wider Caribbean. The site will:

  • Provide resources for local businesses in tourism and trade.
  • Connect entrepreneurs with investors and international partners.
  • Showcase cultural events and festivals.
  • Encourage sustainable tourism and eco-friendly practices.

Audience

  • Local Businesses: Hotels, restaurants, artisans, exporters.
  • Tourists & Visitors: Looking for authentic Caribbean experiences.
  • Investors & Trade Partners: Exploring opportunities in Dominican and Caribbean markets.
  • Community Members: Interested in cultural events and chamber initiatives.

Branding

Logo: A stylized wave and palm tree symbolizing Caribbean tourism and trade.

Colors:

Ocean Blue (#0077b6)
Sunset Orange (#f77f00)
Sand Beige (#f4e1d2)

Fonts:

  • Headings: Montserrat (modern, bold)
  • Body: Open Sans (clean, readable)

Content Outline

  • Home: Attention-grabbing welcome section, community highlights, weather widget, calls to action, and business spotlights.
  • Discover: Local history, current demographics, and upcoming events. Includes an image montage showcasing the area and its attractions.
  • Join: Information about membership benefits and a signup form.
  • Directory: A searchable list of local businesses and organizations that are members of the chamber.
